Methodology

Data source: omgbbqhaxx/BTC-Trading-Since-2020 — 816 net-long BTC entry days from a verified 52x BitMEX trader (Paul Wei / @coolish).

State machine: Kotegawa-inspired 5-state model (QUIET → IMBALANCE_FORMING → IMBALANCE_CONFIRMED → EXTENDED → INVALID). Evaluates range compression, 30W MA flatness, volatility percentile, breakout confirmation, and candle body size.

Alignment: CONFIRMED_MATCH = trader entered on/near an IMBALANCE_CONFIRMED day. PARTIAL_MATCH = entry during QUIET or FORMING state. NO_MATCH = entry during INVALID/EXTENDED.

Limitations: Regime score uses degraded macro data (historical FRED/DXY/VIX not reconstructed in this backtest). Social volume data unavailable historically. Forward returns are simple close-to-close, not accounting for leverage or position management.
